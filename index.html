Long duration capital for complex structures.
We go where conventional debt and equity cannot.
Our capabilities are sophisticated, but our philosophy is simple: long-term success requires long-term partnerships.
Alignment and mutual trust support durable results in complex structures.

2065 Capital provides long-duration, flexible capital in between traditional debt and common equity. With a 12-year track record within The Pritzker Organization, the firm was established as a separate business in 2025 to continue managing its existing portfolio and to support growth. The firm pursues a hybrid strategy across industries and asset classes, partnering with management teams and sponsors to structure durable, aligned investments designed to compound after-tax value over a generational horizon.
